## Video Types

### Type 1: Destination Overview (2-4 minutes)
One per destination. The YouTube version of the destination page.

**Structure:**
1. Opening aerial shot (5-10 sec) â€” hero video clip
2. Quick intro: "Here's what you need to know about [destination]"
3. Getting there (15-20 sec)
4. Top 3 things to do (30-45 sec each, use immersive break clips)
5. Where to eat â€” 2-3 picks with prices (20-30 sec)
6. Where to stay â€” 2-3 picks with prices (20-30 sec)

8. CTA: "Full guide with prices and directions on our site â€” link in description"

**Editing approach:** Stock footage montage with text overlays (destination name, prices, tips). Voiceover by Scott or text-only with background music. No face cam required initially.

**Naming:** `[Destination] Travel Guide 2026 â€” Honest Tips & Real Prices`

**Editing:** Compilation of clips from multiple destinations with comparison graphics.

### Type 3: YouTube Shorts (15-60 seconds)

**Why Shorts matter for a new channel:** Shorts get pushed by the algorithm regardless of subscriber count. They're your fastest path to visibility. ### Month 1: Establish Presence
- **Upload 15+ videos** (5 destination overviews + 10 Shorts)
- YouTube needs content to understand what your channel is about
- Shorts are your discovery engine â€” the algorithm pushes them regardless of sub count
- Set up all playlists, channel layout, and branding before first upload

### Month 2-3: Build Consistency
- **2-3 uploads per week** on a consistent schedule
- Engage with comments immediately (first hour matters for algorithm)
- Post community tab updates when new written guides go live on the site
- Cross-post Shorts to Instagram Reels and TikTok (same content, wider reach)

### Month 3-6: Optimize
- Check YouTube Analytics: which videos get search impressions? Double down on those destinations
- Create "response" videos to trending Philippines travel searches
- Collaborate with Filipino travel creators (comment on their videos first, build relationship)
- Target **1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours** (YouTube Partner Program threshold)

### What NOT to do
- Don't buy subscribers or use sub-for-sub schemes
- Don't upload inconsistently (3 videos one week, nothing for a month)
- Don't ignore Shorts â€” they're the fastest growth lever for new channels
- Don't use the @scottmurray8876 personal channel for any Discover Philippines content
