**Preview specs:** Use hero clip → compress to 720p, 8 sec, no audio, 1-3 MB

**File naming:** `{destination}-hero.mp4` / `{destination}-break-{section}.mp4` / `{destination}-preview.mp4`
**Put files in:** `public/videos/destinations/`
**Save originals to:** `youtube/raw/` (for YouTube editing later)

**After downloading:** See `POST-DOWNLOAD-WORKFLOW.md` for rename/compress/deploy steps.
